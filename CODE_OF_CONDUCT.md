# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Acceptable behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
